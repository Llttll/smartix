Discover Smartix

Who are we?

A student club from the Mechatronics Engineering department at the Technical University. We are a team consisting of friends from different academic stages, united by passion.

Why Smartix?

Inspired by smart systems in engineering. We exist to bridge the gap between theoretical study and practical application through fun, hands-on collaboration.

Our Goals

To build a community that innovates in Arduino & Electronics, supports each other academically, and prepares members for the future of tech.
